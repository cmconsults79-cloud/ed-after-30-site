Inside the ED After 30 list

You are not signing up for a pill promo list. You are signing up for structured clarity about what’s happening with your erections and your health.

  • Foundations of ED – how erections actually work.
  • How ED links to heart health, diabetes and hormones.
  • Simple frameworks to reflect on your own situation.
  • Beta invites for the ED Assessment Journey.

Important: ED After 30 does not currently diagnose, treat or prescribe. All information is educational and is not a substitute for individual medical advice.

Who this is for

Men who want clarity before “quick fixes”.

ED After 30 is for men who prefer understanding and long-term thinking over panic purchases and questionable supplements.

It’s for you if:

  • You’ve noticed changes in your erections and want to know why.
  • You suspect stress, health or hormones are involved.
  • You want to talk to your GP or specialist feeling prepared.
  • You’re open to lifestyle changes, not just tablets.
  • You appreciate clear explanations, not fear or hype.

It’s not a good fit if:

  • You are looking for instant prescriptions or “magic” cures.
  • You want graphic or explicit content.
  • You are in an emergency situation (e.g. chest pain, collapse).
  • You are unwilling to seek personalised medical advice when needed.

ED After 30 works best as a quiet space to think clearly and then act more confidently with your own clinician.

What’s coming

The ED Assessment Journey (early-access first).

Early-access members will be the first to try a guided ED assessment journey designed to help you understand your pattern of symptoms and generate a personalised starting plan.

What it will cover

  • Foundations: how erections work and why ED appears.
  • Symptoms and context: what’s changed and when.
  • Lifestyle and medical factors: sleep, weight, blood pressure, meds.
  • Hormonal and psychological patterns.
  • Your personalised starting plan – including lifestyle focus points, recommended blood tests to discuss with your GP, and practical next-step ideas.

How early-access works

  • You’ll get updates as sections of the journey are built.
  • Some members may be invited to test early drafts.
  • Your feedback will help shape the final version.
  • You’re never obliged to take part in anything that doesn’t suit you.

The aim is not to rush you into a programme but to build something genuinely useful, step by step, with your input.
