ED Assessment Journey

A guided, personalised pathway designed to help you understand your ED pattern and receive a starting plan aligned with how ED is approached in real clinical practice.

1. Foundations Check

Identify onset pattern, severity, morning erections and libido changes.

2. Cardiovascular & Medical Profile

Explore metabolic and vascular factors that commonly contribute to ED.

3. Hormonal Clarity

Recognise patterns that may warrant testosterone or thyroid discussion with a GP.

4. Psychological & Relational Mapping

Understand how stress, anxiety, porn and relationships interact with ED.

5. Erection Quality Patterning

Distinguish physical vs psychological drivers through pattern recognition.

6. Personalised Starting Plan

  • Lifestyle focus points
  • Recommended blood tests to discuss with your GP
  • Practical next-step ideas
  • Optional future support when available
